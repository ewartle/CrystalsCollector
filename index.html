Welcome!


At the start of the game, you will see a random number in the box above the crystals. By clicking on one of the four crystals displayed, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.


Enjoy the game!
